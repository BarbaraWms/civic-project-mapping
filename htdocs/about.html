The Community Capital Fund was created to support and promote innovative and measurable community development in Kansas City, MO.

This site serves to provide a geographical representation of projects funded by CCF so that others may learn about nearby projects and other organizations working in similar areas. CCF believes that neighborhoods that partner with and learn from others doing similar work will have greater capacity, opportunities, and resources to carry out their work and will have a greater impact on community development and revitalization.
